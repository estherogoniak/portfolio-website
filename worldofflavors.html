Let me introduce you to an app that I've developed, focused on featuring genuine recipes from diverse global cuisines. In this project, I've handled everything from conceptualization to designing the user experience (UX) and user interface (UI). With this app, users can not only explore authentic recipes but also mark their cooking adventures on a digital map and easily share their culinary progress with friends. What sets this app apart is its inclusivity, as it caters to a wide range of dietary preferences and restrictions.

structure of procedure

user personas and user stories

First, I created user personas based on the big five personality types. These all want to use a recipe app, but have different ideas and needs. Since the app is intended for a wide range of users, the application should be able to address as many of these needs as possible.

low and mid fidelity wireframes

With these needs in mind, I created the initial designs as low and mid fidelity wireframes. The usability is in the foreground here.

moodboard

For the visual implementation I created a moodboard on which the interface is designed. This way I guarantee a consistent image.

high fidelity wireframes and user testing

I merged UX and UI into high fidelity wireframes. The screens were tested on real users and feedback was gathered afterwards. After evaluating the feedback, the screens were customized according to user preferences.

final mockups

The result: an application that combines the most diverse needs and works on all devices!
