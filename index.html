projects

Made in Cologne

Made in Cologne is a mobile app of the fictitious Cologne museum of the same name. It combines the functions and content of a website, a museum guide and an online store. To provide an optimal experience for all visitors in the museum, the design focused on Android and iOS specific design.

World of Flavors

The World of Flavors app shows its users the diversity of the world's culinary arts. It includes authentic recipes from all over the world with simple instructions. In addition, users can network and exchange ideas about the recipes. Those who like a bit of a challenge can even cook their way around the world and share their successes with friends!
