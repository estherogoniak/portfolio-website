Here, I'd like to introduce my approach to working on the gift recommendation app, which I designed from concept to user experience (UX) and user interface (UI). The app's purpose is to provide users with the perfect gift recommendations by applying various filters and preferences. Its ultimate goal is to deliver personalized gift suggestions for a wide range of recipients..

structure of procedure

user personas and user stories

Based on the Big 5 personality types, I created personas who want to use the product in a variety of ways. This gave me a picture of the features needed to work as realistically as possible.

low and mid fidelity wireframes

I created loose and mid fidelity wireframes that focus on the most important features and user experience.

moodboard

The moodboard, on the other hand, focuses on the basic style of the interface. To create a coherent design for all screens, I have created the guidelines for the UI here.

high fidelity wireframes and user testing

In the high fidelity wireframes, I merged the UX and UI and made adjustments where necessary so that the two work seamlessly together. These designs were tested on real users and adjusted again after their feedback. This way, the realistic design of the application is still guaranteed.

final mockups

And this is the result! On the final mockups you can see how the product looks on the different devices.
