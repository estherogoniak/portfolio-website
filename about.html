About

My Story

I started my professional career as a speech and language therapist and was allowed to gain so much experience in working with people in a very Needs-oriented way. More and more, however, I realized that I see my future in design. I have always enjoyed dealing with aesthetics. As a UI designer I can combine my competences in the exchange with team and customers as well as my creative skills in a wonderful way. I look forward to every project and the new possibilities that arise!

In my free time I like to deal with fashion and interior design. My closet and my apartment are therefore my permanent projects, which I really like to indulge in. I listen to music almost always and everywhere and love to discover new tracks. The only thing missing from my happiness is probably the dog by my side...
